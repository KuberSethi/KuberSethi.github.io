About me

Hi! I'm Kuber Sethi, a Rising Senior at Rutgers University New Brunswick.


I'm currently interning at Amazon Web Services, and have also interned at Ancestry last summer. I'm heavily involved with the CS community at Rutgers. Last year, I was the President of Rutgers Fizzbuzz, one of the biggest CS organizations on campus, which is responsible for helping students with their careers. I was also the Hacker Ambassador for USACS, the main CS organization on campus, my sophomore year, where I helped organize inter-school hackathon relations. Aside from work and school, I'm really passionate about working on side projects. I've recently been having a lot of fun with Go, and am working on an information management tool to help me organize my consumption of information from online articles to my Kindle books.

I strongly believe that everyone should engage in a lifelong pursuit of knowledge, regardless of what knowledge they desire. For me, I've passionate about history, philosophy, current events, and music. If you share similar hobbies, get in touch! I would love to discuss them with someone from a similar background.
